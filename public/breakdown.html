Nvidia Announces New Groundbreaking Quantum GPU

Johnny B. Goode - August 27, 2017

A part of their recent effort to gain an even stronger grasp on the professional market, Nvidia has unvealed a new concept GPU using quantum computing. Aimed at the scientific community, a community Nvida has been notably absent in due to specialized machines already existing, they hope this compact, scalable piece of quantum power will be the step that makes them a name in every lab. When asked for comment, Nvida CEO Jensen Huang stated, "It's like a flux capacitor but for computers." After the announcement, Nvida stock rose .05%.
